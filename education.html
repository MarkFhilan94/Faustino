Hello!

WELCOME! This is the page about my Educational Background, where this page explains about the start of my education in which i wasn't able to add my nursery and kindergarten due to lack of informations.

Elementary

The picture is the School(Nabua Central Pilot School) where I spend my 6years in Elementary, during that 6years was like a roller coaster ride where every grade level I met new classmates and some other that are already familiar faces. This school was somehow part of my childhood memories where I spend 8hrs of my weekdays listening to lectures, doing activities and projects but the most enjoyable part was during the times when we play games.

Junior High School

Nabua National High School was where I spend my 4years in Junior High and 2years in Senior High. Junior High was the most memorable time I had even though I belong to a star section in Junior High, the people in the picture was still my Friends where we still have a communication to one another.

Senior High School

Senior High was the time where I was able to meet new classmates that graduated from different schools but it was also the shortest time I had studying since it is only 10 months and by the end of the semester of grade 11 the pandemic started which was expected to last only by 2 weeks but become 2 months. So we spend grade 12 at home doing modular and also do an online graduation in which we are the only batch that experienced it.

College

How time flies so fast that I will already become a 3rd year college at Bicol University, being able to attend in this University was very unexpected for me since I was not expecting of being able to pass the BUCESS, but after hearing that I passed, I convinced my parents that I want to attend in this University since it was only a one time chance and it was totally worth it.
